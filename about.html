Our Journey

Our History

Founded in 1995, Greenfield State University (formerly Salsal Technical Institute) started as a small technical school with only 50 students. Over the decades, it has grown into a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence, character building, and research innovation. Today, we serve thousands of students across multiple disciplines and continue to lead in technological advancements.

Our Mission

To nurture learners to become responsible, skilled, and compassionate individuals through world-class instruction, innovative research, and active community engagement.

Our Vision

To be a leading global community of innovators and ethical leaders who will make a significant positive impact on society by the year 2030.

The Greenfield Standard

Core Values

  • Excellence: Maintaining the highest standards in education.
  • Integrity: Honesty and transparency in all actions.
  • Compassion: Deep concern for the community and environment.
  • Innovation: Driving change through creativity and technology.

Strategic Goals

  • Modernization of campus laboratories and digital facilities.
  • Strengthening international academic partnerships.
  • Achieving 100% passing rate in professional board exams.
  • Implementing sustainable "Green Campus" initiatives.
